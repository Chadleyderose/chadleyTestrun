About us

Unexpected Opportunities, is a non-profit organisation that is set out to bring about change in the communities. Through feeding, empowering and developing the children, young people and the elderly. Our hearts and passion is to re-build and restore the communities to its former glory. There is much potential in our communities. Due to crime and gangsterism in our areas, this seems to go unnoticed.

We are offering the children and youth various disciplines and activities including Academic Support, Sport, Environmental and Life Skills. Each child will be receiving a meal daily and if we raise sufficient funds, outings through the year. As many children, have not been outside of their communities. We will also be offering the elderly support, as many of them are left stranded. No support whether it be financial or food from their families.
